# Daily Epsilon of Math Calender

This is a calender that offers you daily math problems for you to take a stab. Solve enough problems and you just might discover an interesting thing about the results you get, only if you pay close enough attention. 

I used to get the problems back when i was still active on twittter from this [account](https://x.com/Daily_Epsilon) but it is no longer active but i link it despite the inactivity just in case you want to try your hands at some of the old problems. I currently get problems from Mastodon through [Percy](https://mathstodon.xyz/@PercyButtons3). 

This project has two goals;
1. Writing a _book_ of sorts containing Daily Epsilon probems that i have worked on in the past as well as my solutions to them for the following reasons: 
 - my benefit and this is after reading an [essay](https://blog.evanchen.cc/2015/03/14/writing/) by  Evan Chen. It would be nice to have a catalogue of problems i have worked on that i can take a look at years down the line.
 - This would force me to learn more math. Something i have been saying i want to do but just keep on putting off. I am not satisfied with my mathematical knowledge. 
 - the benefits of my kids as well. 
 > "Dad, these solutions of yours are terrible" \
 > "Yh, i know"
 
Those will be fun conversations to have with my kids that is if they are mathematically inclined. 

2. Learning [Typst](https://typst.app/). I imagine i would be doing a masters in the future. Something with lots of math in it so having this would be nice because i would have had lots of practice with it. This is the future. Forget LaTex. Hehe!
